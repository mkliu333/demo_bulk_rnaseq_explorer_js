⚠️ Method Limitations

  • CPM and log2CPM are calculated directly in the browser for exploratory analysis only.
  • DESeq2-style size-factor normalization in this browser-only app is inspired by the DESeq2 median-of-ratios concept, but it is not equivalent to running DESeq2 in R.
  • approximate VST is a simplified transformation and is NOT equivalent to R DESeq2::vst().
  • For publication-level RNA-seq analysis, validate results using DESeq2, edgeR, or limma-voom in R.
